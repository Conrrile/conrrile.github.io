About

I am a research master's student at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Universiti Sains Malaysia, advised by Assoc. Prof. Dr. Mohd Tahir Ismail. My research focuses on self-supervised representation learning for time series, with an emphasis on spectral methods, contrastive learning, and physics-inspired dynamics modeling. I am also interested in model-based reinforcement learning.

Previously, I earned my B.Sc. in Finance from Shanghai University of International Business and Economics (SUIBE), where my undergraduate thesis explored gold volatility prediction using bidirectional LSTM under the COVID-19 pandemic.

Research Interests

  • Time Series Representation Learning: Self-supervised contrastive learning (CoST, TS2Vec, SoftCLT, TimesURL, SimMTM, PatchTST). Dynamic modeling, spectral mining, spectral distillation.
  • Model-Based Reinforcement Learning: SAC, PPO, DrQ, Deep Bisimulation for Control, Dreamer, World Models.
